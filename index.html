Bronze C++

Level 1, for Beginners. For students who have recently learned to program, but who have no training in algorithms beyond basic concepts like sorting and binary search.

Silver C++

(Future Course) For students who are beginning to learn fundamental problem-solving techniques (e.g., recursive search, greedy algorithms) and fundamental data structures.

Gold C++

(Future Course) Where students encounter more standard algorithms of a more complex nature (e.g., shortest paths, dynamic programming) and more advanced data structures.
