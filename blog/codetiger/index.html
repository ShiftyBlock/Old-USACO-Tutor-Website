Day 0

October 17, 2020.

Honestly, I don't know why I'm restarting writing blogs.

It's not as if I can go write a blog, and suddenly I'm going to improve. But honestly going with the "flow" is literally nonsense for me, I don't touch USACO when I'm in a fucking flow.

But, over the past 2 months, of being "relaxed", the following things have happened:

  • Parents call me lazy
  • I think I am lazy
  • I start up a trash tutoring service
  • I didn't improve
  • Even Brian Dean wants me to get better
  • I also really want to make camp this year and its not happening with the flow

Honestly, I really love USACO but I also hate it so much when it's hard. I don't know how to perservere. Everyone around me: CodeTiger, Fishy15, even Elephant who's so bunched up with school work, are accelerating like rockets.

Just looking at your decision to do Edgenuity, what was it all for? Did you do it to make some nonsense money? Did you do it to waste around, watching youtube? Or did you do it because you had a dream, and you wanted to work for that dream? It's stupid to have no school and do nothing with that time. I really thought you were better than that.

Well, this is your first day. But more like an introduction. Day 0.

I think some important things to note on your odyssey:

  • Don't Dwell on Your Failures. Move ON.
  • It's OK to ask for help
  • Reading Editorial > Quitting
  • William Lin is still better than you
  • If you want it enough, you'll work hard enough.

I think, all in all, I've learned a lot from my first intensive thing, and it was really hard. Now, I know its way easier said than done, but still. I believe in you. Just, don't give up. Don't let me (and BDean) down.
